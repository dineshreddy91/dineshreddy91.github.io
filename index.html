I am a graduate student at Robotic Institute, School of Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University under the supervision of Prof. Srinivasa Narasimhan in ILIM lab. I was supported by the Amazon Go PHD Fellowship

My areas of interest are 3D Vision Computer Vision combined with Machine Learning for Robotics applications. I was fortunate to have collaborations with Laurent Guigues, Leonid Pischulini and Jayan Eledath at Amazon Go, with Dr.Andreas Gieger at MPI-Tubingen and Prof.K Madhava Krishna at IIIT-Hyderabad. Before joining the ever advancing research community, I was a carefree undergraduate at BITS-Pilani working on simple robotics under the guidance of Prof. Suman Kapur
